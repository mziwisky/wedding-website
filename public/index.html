OUR STORY
This story started a little over 4 years ago at Marquette University in Milwaukee, WI. Michael was pursuing his masters degree in engineering and I was pursuing mine in physician assistant studies. His engineering lab was located in Cudahy Hall on the same floor I liked to study on. He approached me one day while I was studying and we had a nice chat. I remember he gave me an organic apple he had bought from a farmer's market. Anybody who knows me knows I'm a bit of a health nut so with a local, organic offering, he was off to a good start. In the coming weeks, I made a point to study more at Cudahy Hall than at home or in the library. And he made a point to come sit down and chat with me. One thing led to another and some study dates led to yoga dates led to a metal concert (yes, that's right. I was trying to show him how open and cool I was going to a metal concert with him) led to a blossoming love that is as strong as ever. I'll leave it at that. Oh wait, actually I'll leave it with one of my favorite Walt Whitman quotes, "We were together. I forget the rest."
